At AJ Hackett Skypark, adventure awaits around every corner! Dive into a world of excitement with our exhilarating activities designed for thrill-seekers and families alike. Whether you’re soaring through the sky on our iconic bungy jumps, experiencing the rush of our giant swings, or gliding along breathtaking ziplines, each experience offers a unique way to challenge yourself and enjoy stunning views. Explore our sky-high attractions, including the Skybridge, Skycube, and the nostalgic Walk The Plank, or engage in fun-filled activities like luge rides, adventure parks, and climbing walls. Whatever your adrenaline level, there’s something for everyone to enjoy at AJ Hackett Skypark—come and create unforgettable memories!
