Acknowledging the Past, Shaping the Future

{% for item in page.reconciliation %}
{% endfor %}

In 2024, AJ Hackett International proudly received endorsement from Reconciliation Australia for our 'Reflect RAP'—a Reconciliation Action Plan that sets the groundwork for meaningful reconciliation initiatives across our business. We honor the Traditional Custodians by recognizing their deep connection to the land and their invaluable knowledge passed down through generations. Our partnership with the Djapugay community at Skypark Cairns serves as a guiding example for our global sites, reinforcing our commitment to sustainability and the protection of our natural environment.
